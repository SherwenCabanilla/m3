Welcome to DOST

Your partner for innovation and progress. We offer a wide range of services to help you achieve your goals, from research and development to technology transfer and commercialization. By signing up with DOST, you'll gain access to a wealth of knowledge and expertise that can help you turn your ideas into reality. Join us today and take the first step towards realizing your dreams.

At DOST, our mission is to harness science and technology for the development of the Philippines, promote innovation and entrepreneurship, and enhance the competitiveness of our industries. Our vision is to be the leading organization in science and technology in the Philippines, providing cutting-edge solutions to the country's most pressing challenges.

We offer a wide range of programs and services to support scientific research, promote innovation and entrepreneurship, and enhance the competitiveness of our industries. Our programs include scholarships, grants, and funding for research and development, as well as training and capacity-building programs for students, researchers, and entrepreneurs.

We have many success stories of individuals and organizations that have benefited from our programs and services. From groundbreaking scientific research to successful startup companies, DOST has helped countless Filipinos achieve their goals and make a positive impact on society.
